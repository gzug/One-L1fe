# Medizin 3.0 Longevity Strategies

## Four Riders Framework
The Four Riders framework is a comprehensive approach to understanding longevity strategies. Incorporating various aspects of health, it includes:
1. **Physical Activity**
2. **Nutrition**
3. **Sleep**
4. **Mental Well-being**

## Biomarker Thresholds
Monitoring specific biomarkers can provide valuable insights into health status. The recommended thresholds are:
- **ApoB**: < 90 mg/dL
- **HbA1c**: < 5.7%
- **CRP**: < 1 mg/L
- **Vitamin D**: 30-50 ng/mL
- **Lp(a)**: < 30 mg/dL

## Training Protocols
Establishing effective training protocols is essential:
- **VO2 max**: Aim for 50-70% of max HR in training.
- **Zone 2**: Training in the optimal fat-burning zone.
- **Strength Training**: Focus on compound movements.
- **Stability**: Incorporate balance exercises.

## Nutrition Guidelines
A balanced nutritional approach is crucial:
- **Protein**: 1.2 to 2.2 g/kg of body weight.
- **Metabolic Control**: Low glycemic index foods.
- **Supplementation**: Consider Omega-3, Vitamin D, and multivitamins.

## Sleep
Prioritize sleep quality:
- Aim for 7-9 hours per night.
- Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.

## Brain Health
Brain health is vital for longevity:
- Engage in cognitive activities such as puzzles and learning.
- Stay socially active to boost mental health.

## Diagnostic Recommendations
Regular checkups are essential:
- Annual blood tests for biomarker monitoring.
- Assess cardiovascular health regularly.
